# AGENTS.md

## Scope
- Applies to files under `presentation/`.
- Prefer deck-local overrides if a subdirectory adds one later.

## Slide Writing
- Titles must be `5` words or fewer.
- One slide should carry `1` main point, or at most `2` tightly related points.
- Prefer short bullets and small tables over dense paragraphs.

## Flow
- Overview slides should frame the next section, not explain its details.
- Detail slides should explain one layer deeper than the surrounding slides, not all layers at once.
- Avoid repeating the same fact in text on multiple slides unless the later slide adds a new implication.

## Structure
- If details have hierarchy, show the hierarchy.
- Do not present all details as one flat list when categories or stages matter.
- When possible, group by `question -> method -> implication`.

## Tables And Figures
- Do not narrate a table or graph row by row.
- Commentary should state the implication, tradeoff, or decision signal.
- Prefer `pattern + implication` over literal restatement of plotted values.

## Style
- Keep wording compact and presentation-like.
- Prefer concrete nouns and verbs over explanatory filler.
- If a point can be inferred from a visual, do not restate it as another long bullet.
